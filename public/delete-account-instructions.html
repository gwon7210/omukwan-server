How to delete your account on Omukwan

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and related data, you have two options:

  1. Email Request: Contact us via email at parkjiwon7210@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  2. In-App Request: Use the "Delete Account" button in the app's settings menu

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your login credentials and authentication information
  • Personal profile information
  • All posts and comments you've created
  • Group memberships and group data
  • Likes and interactions
  • Uploaded images and media files
  • Notification preferences and history
  • Any other user-generated content

Deletion Timeline

Your data will be removed within 7 days of confirmation. During this period:

  • Your account will be marked for deletion
  • You can still cancel the deletion request
  • After 7 days, all data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered
⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure you have backed up any important data before proceeding.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about account deletion or need assistance, please contact us:

Email: support@omukwan.com

Response Time: We typically respond within 24-48 hours
